About

Global Virtual Studio for Anime Production

VILOSTUDIOS is a global virtual studio with integrated sound partner HEX ARCHIVE. Founded in 2022 by industry veterans from Solo Leveling, One Piece, and Cyberpunk Edgerunners, we operate through a distributed network of freelance professionals worldwide.

Our Mission

Achieve self-sustainability through producing our own original shows while revolutionizing anime production. We develop proprietary tools and workflows specifically designed for anime pipelines, making production more efficient and accessible.

Virtual Studio Model

Our freelancer database allows us to constantly resupply our workforce with top-tier animators and sound designers globally. We build custom teams around each project, connecting professionals across time zones for seamless remote collaboration.

Focused Approach

We take up to 3 main client projects annually, prioritizing quality over quantity. This focused approach allows us to dedicate resources to developing our own IPs while delivering exceptional work for partners in 2D and 3D animation.
